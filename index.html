It's never too early to start learning!

At Indigenous Coders Collective (ICC), we believe in the power of early education to shape the future. Our mission is to empower indigenous youth by providing them with the tools and training they need to succeed in the digital world. Through our three-week summer camp program, we offer hands-on learning experiences in web development, covering essential skills in HTML, CSS, and more. By fostering a supportive and inclusive environment, ICC aims to bridge the digital divide and inspire the next generation of indigenous tech leaders. Join us in nurturing creativity, innovation, and confidence—because it's never too early to start learning!

Program

Our three-week summer camp is designed to introduce indigenous youth to the exciting world of web development. Participants will gain hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, learning how to build and design their own websites from scratch. Our goal is to spark a passion for technology and provide the foundational skills needed for future success.

Daily Schedule

Each day at the camp is filled with engaging and interactive activities. Mornings start with coding lessons and practice, followed by collaborative projects and problem-solving exercises. Afternoons include guest speakers, workshops, and creative sessions where participants can apply what they've learned. The day concludes with group discussions and feedback to reinforce learning and encourage growth.

Resources

We provide all the necessary tools and resources to ensure a successful learning experience. Each participant will receive a laptop, access to high-speed internet, and all required software for coding. In addition, we offer a variety of learning materials, including tutorials, guides, and project templates to support and enhance their educational journey.

Mentorship

Our program is supported by a team of dedicated mentors and instructors who are passionate about technology and education. Each participant will have access to personalized guidance and support throughout the camp. Our mentors provide valuable insights, answer questions, and help troubleshoot any challenges, ensuring that every student feels confident and empowered in their learning process.
